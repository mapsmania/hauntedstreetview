Nightmare on View Street

Click on the map to identify the location of the haunted Street View scene.

There are 5 haunted Street Views to find in each game.

Street View has become haunted by a strange mix of ghosts, ghouls and monsters.

  • In this game you will be presented with a series of five images of haunted Street Views from around the world. Your task is to locate each Street View on the map by clicking on its location.

  • At the end of the game you will see a summary with the distances between your locations and the correct positions.

Game developed by Maps Mania, based on Backdrop and optimised for mobile by Geoendevina
